# Project Brief: Pony Club Ecotourism

## 1. Project Name
Pony Club Ecotourism

## 2. Project Type
Next.js web application for a tour operator company.

## 3. Core Objective
To create a modern, engaging, and informative online presence for Pony Club Ecotourism. The platform will showcase the company's tours and activities, provide detailed information to potential customers, and facilitate inquiries or bookings.

## 4. Target Audience
- Individuals, families, and groups interested in ecotourism activities such as horse riding, kayaking, rafting, trekking, and ziplining.
- Schools or organizations looking for outdoor educational programs.
- Tourists seeking unique and environmentally conscious travel experiences.

## 5. Key Goals
- **Information Hub:** Serve as the primary source of information for all tours, activities, and services offered by Pony Club Ecotourism.
- **Showcase Experiences:** Visually appeal to users by showcasing the beauty and excitement of the ecotourism experiences.
- **User Engagement:** Encourage users to explore offerings, learn about the company's commitment to ecotourism, and contact for more details or bookings.
- **Brand Building:** Establish a strong online brand identity that reflects the company's values of sustainability, adventure, and quality.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure the website is accessible and user-friendly across various devices and for users with disabilities.
- **Scalability:** Build a platform that can grow with the company, allowing for easy addition of new tours, content, and features.

## 6. Scope
### Initial Scope (MVP):
- Homepage showcasing key activities and company mission.
- Individual pages for each main activity (e.g., Riding, Kayaking, Rafting, Trekking, Ziplining, River Village, For Schools).
- Detailed information on each activity page: description, what's included, pricing (if applicable), gallery.
- Contact information and inquiry form.
- Basic SEO setup.
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- Integration with Bokun for widgets/booking (as suggested by existing files).
- Language selection (English and Greek, as suggested by existing `lib/translations`).

### Future Considerations (Post-MVP):
- Online booking system (if not fully covered by Bokun integration).
- Blog or news section for updates and articles related to ecotourism.
- Customer testimonials or review section.
- Advanced SEO and marketing integrations.
- Interactive maps for tour locations.
- User accounts for managing bookings or preferences.

## 7. Success Metrics
- Increased website traffic and user engagement (e.g., time on site, pages per visit).
- Growth in inquiries and bookings originating from the website.
- Positive user feedback on website usability and information clarity.
- Improved search engine rankings for relevant keywords.
- High performance scores (Core Web Vitals).
