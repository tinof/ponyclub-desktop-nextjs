# Product Overview

Pony Club is a tourism website for adventure activities in Acheron River, Greece. The business offers outdoor experiences including rafting, horse riding, kayaking, and trekking in the scenic Glyki area.

## Key Features

- Multilingual support (English/Greek) with i18n routing
- Online booking integration via Bokun widgets
- GDPR-compliant consent management with c15t
- Performance-optimized with static generation
- Google Analytics and conversion tracking
- Responsive design with vintage/rustic aesthetic
- SEO-optimized with structured data

## Target Audience

- Tourists visiting Greece seeking outdoor adventures
- Families looking for safe, guided activities
- Adventure enthusiasts interested in river sports
- International visitors (primary English, secondary Greek)

## Business Goals

- Drive online bookings through integrated widgets
- Improve search visibility for adventure tourism keywords
- Provide seamless multilingual user experience
- Maintain GDPR compliance for EU visitors
